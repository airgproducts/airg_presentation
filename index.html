About airg

In 2009 AIRG was founded by Richard Gallon,
one of the very early Paragliding and Acro-Paragliding Legends.
His acrobatic glider, the Emilie, is leading the worldcup-ranking since then and his company became market-leader in a very short time.

AIRG is known for the relaxed atmosphere and the inspirational lifestyle of Richard.
He gave his company to two young pilots from Innsbruck to ensure it is being continued in his spirit. To make sure it is driven by passion, not profit.
